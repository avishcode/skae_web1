Founder & Director message

Empowering Communication

Dear All,
At the outset, I thank you for your interest in getting associated with Skae – One of the leading spoken English centers in Eastern U.P. Our power of discourse is one of the most incisive ways to express our personality. No one has the ability to ascertain what we contemplate/muse/dwell upon, our sentiments,wit and diligence by merely see our face.It can only be understood the way we communicate or the way we express our ideas in front of them. The one who secures 80-90% can be unsuccessful if he fails to deliver his ideas or views effectively while facing an interview. On the contrary, the one who secures 50-60% can be triumphant if he knows how to expound his ideas in an impressive manner.
Whoever has become a great leader or motivator in the millennia, it is due to their communication skills, be it in the field of business or in the area of politics. We firmly believe that empowering communication plays the major role in overall Personality Development.
